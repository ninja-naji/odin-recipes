Stewed Dominican Salami

What is Stewed Dominican Salami (Salami Guisado)?

Made in one pan, Dominican salami and fresh veggies are cooked in a rich and flavorful tomato sauce for one hearty stew. It takes minutes to prep, made with simple ingredients, and itâ€™s ready to serve in less than half an hour.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Red Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Dried Oregano
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Dominican Salami
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lime Juice to Serve

Steps

  1. In a saute pan, heat vegetable oil over medium high heat. Add onion and bell pepper. Cook until the onion cooks through and becomes translucent, about 2 minutes.
  2. Stir in chicken bouillon and tomato sauce. Cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Add salami and stir to combine. Continue to cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in the water and let simmer for about 10 minutes or until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice before serving.
